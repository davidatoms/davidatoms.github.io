Snowflake Data Privacy Policy

Data Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2026

Updated: January 29, 2026

1. Scope and Purpose

This Privacy Policy governs the data products (the "Data") provided by dammastock, L.L.C. ("Provider") via the Snowflake Marketplace. This policy outlines how we handle information and the standards we maintain for the data we sell.

2. Data Sourcing and Compliance

We are committed to the ethical and legal sourcing of data. We represent and warrant that the Data provided:

  • Anonymization: Has been aggregated or de-identified such that it does not constitute "Personal Information" under CCPA/CPRA or "Personal Data" under GDPR, or;
  • Lawful Basis: Has been collected under a valid legal basis with all necessary consents and permissions for commercial redistribution.
  • Prohibited Content: Does not contain "Protected Health Information" (PHI) as defined by HIPAA or sensitive financial identifiers, unless explicitly stated in the listing documentation.
  • Listing-Specific Terms: Individual data listings may include supplemental terms that supersede or modify this policy. Review each listing's documentation for data-specific disclosures.

3. Data Access and Collection Limits

The Snowflake Marketplace architecture ensures a "privacy-by-design" relationship.

  • No Access to Consumer Environment: Provider has no access to your Snowflake account, your underlying data, or the queries you run against our Data.
  • No PII Collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, or payment details from Consumers. All billing and identity management are handled exclusively by Snowflake Inc.
  • Provider Visibility: Through Snowflake's Provider Studio, we receive consumer organization names, account locators, and contact information for individuals who access our listings, as well as usage metrics (queries executed, active consumers). This information is used solely for service improvement and support purposes.
  • Data Retention: Consumer contact information received through Snowflake is retained for the duration of the subscription plus [X] years for audit and compliance purposes.

4. Data Security and Integrity

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to ensure the Data is accurate, secure, and free from malicious code. However, the Data is provided on an "As-Is" basis, and we do not guarantee that the Data will be error-free or uninterrupted.

5. Consumer Obligations

By subscribing to our Data, the Consumer agrees to:

  • No Re-Identification: Refrain from any attempt to re-identify individuals or households from the Data.
  • Lawful Use: Use the Data only for lawful purposes and in compliance with all applicable local, state, and international regulations.
  • Attribution: Adhere to any attribution requirements specified in the individual listing.

6. Third-Party Data Processing

Snowflake Inc. acts as an independent service provider. Consumers are encouraged to review the Snowflake Privacy Notice to understand how Snowflake manages Marketplace transaction data and metadata.

7. Updates to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy to reflect changes in our data practices or legal obligations. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page will indicate when the last changes were made.
